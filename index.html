Frequently Asked

How do you verify a project before listing it? +

Our legal desk reviews land title, RERA registration, and builder track record before any listing goes live to clients.

Do you assist NRI clients remotely? +

Yes — including power-of-attorney coordination, virtual site tours, and remote registry support.

Is home loan assistance included? +

We coordinate directly with partner banks and NBFCs to pre-approve financing before you shortlist properties.

What are your service charges? +

Consultation is complimentary. Brokerage is disclosed upfront with no hidden fees, discussed during your first call.
